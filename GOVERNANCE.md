# Governance

## Decision Making

All project decisions are made by the organization owner. The owner may delegate review authority to trusted contributors at their discretion.

## Roles

### Organization Owner

The owner has final authority over all projects, direction, and governance changes.

### Core Reviewers

Core reviewers are contributors granted review and merge rights on repositories within the organization. Core reviewers are added by the owner and may be removed at any time. There are no formal criteria for becoming a core reviewer — inclusion is based solely on the owner's trust and judgment.

### Contributors

Contributors are members of the [OSK contributor team](https://github.com/orgs/OpenSourceKingdom/teams) who have read/write access to repositories. To become a contributor:

1. Be an active, trusted contributor to the organization
2. Request membership in the OSK contributor team directly from the owner (via Discord)
3. Approval is at the owner's sole discretion

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Contributors may be asked to affirm a CLA before their PRs are merged. The CLA process is handled directly through Discord with the organization owner.

## Governance Changes

This governance model may be updated by the organization owner at any time without notice.
